## Codebase Patterns
- Battle Frontier Master cycle point data lives in `data/battle-frontier-master-points.csv` with a `speciesId,points` header and should stay aligned with the active bundled PvPoke cup tier rules while using canonical ids from `data/pokemon.json`.
- Battle Frontier Master shadow point inheritance belongs in code, not the CSV: inherit a listed base species cost only when the shadow variant exists in `data/pokemon.json` and is ranked for `battle-frontier-master`.
- In `app/api/generate-team/route.ts`, validate Battle Frontier Master anchor legality after ranked-species and uniqueness checks but before `generateTeam(...)`, and map the first legality violation to a deterministic HTTP 400 message.
- In `lib/genetic/chromosome.ts`, enforce Battle Frontier Master initialization legality while filling each slot and in the random fallback path, then keep a final legality assertion on the completed team so later operator work can reuse the same pattern.
- In `lib/genetic/operators.ts`, thread `formatId` through `createNextGeneration(...)` into `crossover(...)` and `mutate(...)`, and fall back to the original legal parent/chromosome whenever Battle Frontier Master operator output would become illegal.
- For battle-format-specific setup feedback, keep `TeamManager` as the owner of transient generation errors and pass them into `TeamConfigPanel` for inline alerts; render static format-rule copy in the config panel instead of duplicating it inside `TeamGenerator`.
